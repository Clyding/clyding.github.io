You can also put regular text between your rows of images. Describe how you toiled, sweated, bled for your project, and then reveal its glory in the next row of images.

You can also have artistically styled 2/3 + 1/3 images, like these.

The code is simple. Just wrap your images with <div class="col-sm"> and place them inside <div class="row">. To make images responsive, add img-fluid class to each; for rounded corners and shadows use rounded and z-depth-1 classes.
