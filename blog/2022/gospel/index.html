The Gospel of John - Plan of Salvation

He has saved us and called us to a holy life—not because of anything we have done but because of his own purpose and grace. This grace was given us in Christ Jesus before the beginning of time II Timothy 1:9

- a Clovis Church of Christ excerpt, CA

What Must I do to be Saved?

This is the question that every man and woman must answer in their lives if they hope to spend eternity with God in Heaven. The apostle Paul in Phil 2:12, addresses the brethren in Philippi;

"Wherefore, my beloved, as ye have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling."

Thus, we understand that it is our own responsibility to answer the question, "What we must do to be saved?" No one else can answer that question for us, nor can we trust others with this, most important, decision. We must seek our answer from God and His Word.

Gospel: The Gospel (Good News) signifies The Death, Burial, and the Resurrection of Christ. The bad news that all are guilty of sin and condemned by God is countered by the gospel, the good news of Jesus Christ. God, because of His love for the world, has made a way for man to be forgiven of their sins ( John 3:16 ). He sent His Son, Jesus Christ, to take the sins of mankind on Himself through death on a cross ( I Peter 2:24 ). In placing our sin on Christ, God ensured that all who will believe in the name of Jesus will be forgiven ( Acts 10:43 ). Jesus’ resurrection guarantees the justification of all who believe ( Rom 4:25 ).

After Sin infiltrated the world, there was a heavy flood cleansing lasting 40 days and 40 nights. "The Plan of Salvation? An Ark, Noah's Ark". Noah Preached to the world for over 120 years , yet only 7 others will yield in obedience.

"For if God did not spare angels when they sinned, but sent them to hell,[a] putting them in chains of darkness[b] to be held for judgment; 5 if he did not spare the ancient world when he brought the flood on its ungodly people, but protected Noah, a preacher of righteousness, and seven others;" II Peter 2:4-5

"The world restarted with 8 righteous men after the flood, from them are all the descendants of the world" [Gen 10:1-32]. This calls for an urgent rekindling of our hearts and minds!

Steps to Salvation

The steps to Salvation are evidently attainable as Paul attests in II Timothy 4:7-8: "I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith. 8 Now there is in store for me the crown of righteousness, which the Lord, the righteous Judge, will award to me on that day—and not only to me, but also to all who have longed for his appearing."

Paul goes on to claim that, there remains a crown of glory for they who proof themselves worthy after running this race of glory.

"23 I do all this for the sake of the gospel, that I may share in its blessings. 24 Do you not know that in a race all the runners run, but only one gets the prize? Run in such a way as to get the prize. 25 Everyone who competes in the games goes into strict training. They do it to get a crown that will not last, but we do it to get a crown that will last forever." [ I Corinthians 9:23-25 ]

  • 1. Hear the Gospel
    • Romans 10:17, John 8:32
  • 2. Believe the Gospel
    • Hebrews 11:6, John 20:31
  • 3. Repent of your old Sins
    • Luke 13:3, Acts 17:30
  • 4. Confess your Faith in Christ
    • Romans 10:10, Matthew 10:32
  • 5. Baptism to Seal
    • Galatians 3:27, Mark 16:16, Acts 2:38
  • 6. Be a Faithful Servant of God
    • Revelation 2:10

Conclusion

The Great Commission: Jesus commands His disciples to preach and spread the Gospel to all the nations. For God demonstrates His love towards us whiles we are still sinners: by sending His only begotten Son to give His life as a randsome and atonement of our sins, that we may gain eternal life and become equal heirs with His son, Jesus Christ. ( Romans 8:17 )

He has also given us the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, to guide us in our spiritual journey John 16:13. God has given us the power to overcome Sin, the devil and all the attrocities the world impose unto Believers. This is the Faith we HAVE!
